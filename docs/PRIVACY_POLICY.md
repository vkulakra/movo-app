# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 31, 2026**

## Introduction

Movo ("we," "our," or "us") is a habit tracker and mood journal app that respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

**Our core principle is simple: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 No Account Required

Movo does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.) to use the App.

### 1.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you enter into the App is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This includes:

- **Habits** — the habits you create and their completion history
- **Mood entries** — your mood logs and journal notes
- **App preferences** — your theme selection (light/dark), reminder settings, and notification preferences
- **Ad-free purchase state** — a flag indicating whether you have purchased the ad-free upgrade

This data never leaves your device. We do not have access to it, nor can we retrieve it if you uninstall the App.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

The free version of the App displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. We have configured AdMob to serve **non-personalized ads only**. This means:

- Google does **not** use your browsing history, app usage, or any personal identifiers to tailor ads to you
- Ads are served based on general factors such as your approximate location (at the city/country level) and the content of the App
- No advertising ID or similar tracking identifier is used for ad personalization

Google's use of information collected through AdMob is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not share any user data with Google beyond what is required for serving non-personalized ads.

### 2.2 Google Play In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase the "Remove Ads" upgrade, the transaction is processed entirely through Google Play's billing system. Google Play handles all payment processing and related data. We do not receive or store any payment information.

For information on how Google handles your payment data, please refer to the [Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/about/play-terms/).

### 2.3 No Other Third-Party Services

The App does **not** integrate any other third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. Specifically, we do not use:

- Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, etc.)
- Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any analytics SDKs
- Facebook SDK or any social media SDKs
- Any form of push notification service (notifications are local only)

---

## 3. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's built-in protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend that you:

- Use a device passcode, pattern, or biometric lock
- Keep your device's operating system up to date
- Use device encryption if supported

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

### 4.1 Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the App installed and do not delete your data within the App.

### 4.2 Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by either:

- Clearing the App's data through your device's application settings
- Uninstalling the App

Once uninstalled, all data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The non-personalized ad configuration is consistent with the requirements of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
