# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2, 2026**

## Introduction

Movo ("we," "our," or "us") is a habit tracker and mood journal app that respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

**Our core principle is simple: Your personal content (habits, mood entries, journal notes, and preferences) stays on your device — we never have access to it. We do collect anonymized usage data via Firebase Analytics to help us understand how the app is used and improve it over time.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 No Account Required

Movo does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.) to use the App.

### 1.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you enter into the App is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This includes:

- **Habits** — the habits you create and their completion history
- **Mood entries** — your mood logs and journal notes
- **App preferences** — your theme selection (light/dark), reminder settings, and notification preferences
- **Ad-free purchase state** — a flag indicating whether you have purchased the ad-free upgrade

This data never leaves your device. We do not have access to it, nor can we retrieve it if you uninstall the App.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

The free version of the App displays banner, native, and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. We have configured AdMob to serve **non-personalized ads only**. This means:

- Google does **not** use your browsing history, app usage, or any personal identifiers to tailor ads to you
- Ads are served based on general factors such as your approximate location (at the city/country level) and the content of the App
- **However**, the Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically accesses your device's **Advertising ID** (a unique, user-resettable identifier provided by the Android operating system) for essential ad-serving functions such as frequency capping, ad measurement, and fraud prevention — even when serving non-personalized ads

Google's use of information collected through AdMob is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not share any user data with Google beyond what is required for serving ads.

### 2.2 Google Play In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase the "Remove Ads" upgrade, the transaction is processed entirely through Google Play's billing system. Google Play handles all payment processing and related data. We do not receive or store any payment information.

For information on how Google handles your payment data, please refer to the [Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/about/play-terms/).

### 2.3 Firebase Analytics (App Usage Analytics)

The App uses Firebase Analytics, an analytics service provided by Google, to help us understand how users interact with the App (e.g., which features are used most). Firebase Analytics collects:

- **Event data** — actions you perform in the App (e.g., logging a mood, completing a habit)
- **Screen views** — which screens you visit
- **Device information** — device model, OS version
- **Advertising ID** — a unique, user-resettable identifier used for analytics measurement and reporting

Firebase Analytics does **not** collect:
- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- Habits, mood entries, or journal content
- Any data stored locally on your device

Firebase Analytics' use of data is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2.4 Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

If you opt in, the App uses Firebase Crashlytics, a crash reporting service provided by Google, to help us improve the stability of the App. Crashlytics collects:

- **Crash logs** — technical data about app crashes (stack traces, device state at time of crash)
- **Device information** — device model, OS version, time of crash

**Crash reporting is opt-in only.** You will be asked on first launch whether you'd like to enable crash reporting. You can change this preference at any time in the App's Settings. If you do not opt in, no crash data is collected.

Crashlytics does **not** collect:
- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- Habits, mood entries, or journal data
- Any data stored locally on your device

Firebase Crashlytics' use of data is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2.5 No Other Third-Party Services

The App does **not** integrate any other third-party services beyond those listed above. Specifically, we do not use:

- Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any other analytics SDKs (beyond Firebase Analytics)
- Facebook SDK or any social media SDKs
- Any form of push notification service (notifications are local only)

## 3. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's built-in protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend that you:

- Use a device passcode, pattern, or biometric lock
- Keep your device's operating system up to date
- Use device encryption if supported

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

### 4.1 Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the App installed and do not delete your data within the App.

### 4.2 Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by either:

- Clearing the App's data through your device's application settings
- Uninstalling the App

Once uninstalled, all data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The non-personalized ad configuration is consistent with the requirements of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

- **Right to access** — Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to access
- **Right to deletion** — Uninstall the App to delete all locally stored data
- **Right to opt out of ads** — The App serves non-personalized ads; no further opt-out is needed for ad personalization
- **Right to opt out of analytics** — You can opt out of Firebase Analytics collection at any time via your device's settings (Android: *Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization*, or reset your Advertising ID). You can also uninstall the App at any time to stop all data collection

To exercise any rights, you may contact us at the email address below.
